Robotics

My journey in robotics started when I was in 6th grade, driven by a deep curiosity for how things work. I began at Dolphin Labs, where I immersed myself in electronics, programming languages, and technologies like Arduino and Raspberry Pi. Working on various projects there laid a strong foundation for my passion in this field. At SVR InfoTech, I had the opportunity to work on a humanoid robot, where I was responsible for programming its locomotion and managing the electronics. Collaborating with a BARC scientist on this project was a significant learning experience that deepened my understanding of robotics. My commitment to self-learning has driven me to constantly explore new technologies and challenges in robotics. Participating in engineering exhibitions across Maharashtra further honed my skills and exposed me to a wider community of like-minded individuals. This passion continues to fuel my work and innovation in robotics.
