My Entrepreneurship Journey

From a young age, I had an entrepreneurial spirit that drove me to explore the world of business. In the 11th grade, I channeled this passion into founding a marketing company alongside a close-knit group of friends. Driven by our collective enthusiasm, we worked with a variety of startups, including Wooddaddy and ShonaByZain, as well as resorts and educational institutes. What began as a side hustle quickly turned into a thriving business, allowing me to gain invaluable experience in the world of marketing and business development.

After taking a brief pause to focus on my studies during the 12th grade, I returned with renewed vigor and founded Nilegenix, a pharma company. This venture marked a new chapter in my entrepreneurial journey, blending my interest in science and business. My journey has always been fueled by a deep curiosity and a desire to learn and grow. Whether it's through my love for robotics and technology or my fascination with finance, neuroscience, and philosophy, I am committed to continuously expanding my knowledge and skills. Every day is an opportunity to learn something new, and I embrace the challenges and adventures that come my way.

    As the founder of Hike Helix, I led the company in delivering tailored marketing solutions to clients in education, hospitality, and startups.   • 2022

    As the Partner and CEO of Nilegenix Pharma, I oversee all executive and day-to-day operations of the company. This role has provided me with the opportunity to dive deep into the nuances and complexities of the Indian market. Every challenge presents a unique learning experience, allowing me to navigate and adapt to the ever-evolving landscape of the pharma industry.
